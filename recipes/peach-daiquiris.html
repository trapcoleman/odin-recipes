Peach Daiquiris!

What a tasty summer drink.

Daiquiris made with fresh summer peaches can be made with rum or with water for a non-alcoholic version. This refreshing cocktail is the perfect accompaniment on those lazy summer evenings. Life’s good while you’re sipping on one of these peach daiquiris

The key is using fresh, ripe peaches. You won’t get the same wonderful peach flavor if you’re using frozen peaches.

Ingredients

  • 3 fresh peaches, peeled and quartered
  • 1/4 C granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz light rum or water
  • 2 C ice

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients in a blender until all ingredients are combined and there are no large chunks of ice. Serve in glasses and garnish with fresh peaches and lime.
